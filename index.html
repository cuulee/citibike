Rebancing study

(что-то про актуальность проблемы balancing)

Inspired by Alexander Tedeschi's master degree thesis «A geospatial analysis of bike share redistribution in New York City» we decided to explore the system data for last year and investigated the routes of trips in workdays for one hour intervals. Also the average avaialability for each hour in workdays was analysed.

Read the story on Urbica's Medium or explore the data in this interactive tool.
